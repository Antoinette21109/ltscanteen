About Literacy Tree Canteen

We are dedicated to providing nutritious, delicious meals that support your child's growth and learning journey.

Our Story

Literacy Tree School Canteen was established in 2015 as part of Literacy Tree School's commitment to holistic child development. We believe that proper nutrition is fundamental to effective learning and overall well-being.

Over the years, we have served thousands of nutritious meals to our students, continuously improving our menu based on nutritional guidelines and feedback from parents and students. Our canteen has become an integral part of the school community, promoting healthy eating habits and providing a social space where children can enjoy their meals together.

Today, we continue to innovate with our online ordering system, making it easier for parents to manage their children's meals while ensuring quality and food safety standards are always met.
