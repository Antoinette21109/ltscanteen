Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our canteen services.

How do I place an order for my child?

You can place an order through our online ordering system. Simply log in to your account, select the menu items you'd like to order, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose from various payment options including mobile money and credit card.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including Airtel Money, MTN Money, Zamtel Money, and Visa card payments. All transactions are secure and you'll receive an email receipt after payment.

Can I order in advance?

Yes, you can place orders up to one week in advance. This is especially helpful for planning meals for the entire week. Simply select the date when placing your order.

What if my child has dietary restrictions?

We accommodate various dietary restrictions. Our menu includes vegetarian options, and we clearly label items containing common allergens. If your child has specific dietary needs, please contact us directly to discuss options.

How do I cancel or modify an order?

You can cancel or modify orders up to 24 hours before the delivery date. Log in to your account, go to your order history, and select the order you wish to modify. For same-day changes, please call us directly.

Do you provide refunds?

Refunds are available for orders cancelled at least 24 hours in advance. In case of any issues with your order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll work to resolve the situation promptly.
